About

Bibingka

Introducing our Bibingka with a twist! A modern take on the classic Filipino rice cake! This beloved holiday favorite is still cooked the traditional way—over charcoals, wrapped in banana leaves, giving it that authentic smoky flavor.


What makes ours unique? We've added a blend of flavors and ingredients that elevate the dish while staying true to its roots. Expect the familiar softness of rice flour, coconut cream, salted egg, and a surprise twist of ingredients like ham and bacon, making every bite a delicious experience.

About

Claypot

Experience a unique, flavorful meal with our Claypot Chicken or Pork Over Rice, served with traditional Filipino dishes, all cooked over charcoal for that authentic, smoky taste.


Our signature dishes include Chicken Caldereta, a rich, tomato-based stew; Pork Estopado, with its sweet and savory balance; Chicken Estopado, slow-cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and caramelized sugar; and the classic Pork Adobo, marinated and simmered to perfection.


The charcoal-cooking method infuses each dish with deep, rich flavors that transport you back to the traditional kitchens of the Philippines.
